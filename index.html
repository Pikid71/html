CER paragraph for 'Thank You M'am'

Claim

In the story “Thank You M'am” by Langston Hughes, Mrs. Jones takes Roger home because she wants to teach him a lesson. On page 3, lines 5 and 6, it states, “...a boy ran up behind her and tried to snatch her purse.” My first piece of evidence supports my claim because stealing a purse is very wrong. On page 4, lines 14 and 15, it states “...picked the boy up, and shook him until his teeth rattled.” My second piece of evidence supports my claim because Roger will never forget the moment he felt like a rattlesnake. On page 9, lines 152 and 153, it states “...do not make the mistake of latching into my pocketbook.” My third piece of evidence supports my claim because she concluded with a sentence that assures he will never make the mistake again. Therefore, Mrs. Jones took Roger home to teach him a lesson.
